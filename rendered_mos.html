MOS Experiment

Synthetic Speech Quality Evaluation

Welcome to our experiment, which is a Mean Opinion Score (MOS) test for evaluating our Text-to-Speech (TTS) system. In this test, you will listen to a series of audio files that contain a mix of both real human recordings and synthesized voices. Please make sure to use headphones for the best experience.

We kindly ask you to rate the quality of each audio file based on the following scale:

  • 1 (Very Bad): The audio is highly distorted, difficult to understand, and unpleasant to listen to.
  • 2 (Bad): The audio has noticeable issues that make it uncomfortable to listen to, though you can still understand the content.
  • 3 (Fair): The audio is somewhat clear, but there are some issues that slightly affect its quality.
  • 4 (Good): The audio is clear and pleasant to listen to, with only minor issues.
  • 5 (Very Good): The audio is exceptionally clear, natural, and pleasant, with no noticeable issues.

Your feedback is crucial for us to improve our TTS system. Thank you for your participation!
